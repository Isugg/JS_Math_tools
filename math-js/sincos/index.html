This is a visualization of the sine and cosine graphs as measured from the x and y coordinates of a circle. Here the blue dot is a circular orbit. Next we can break it down into the x and y coordinates of each position of the blue dot. These are the yellow dots. If we expand the yellow dots, we get the sine and cosine functions!
